Urban Sprawl

Dec 2013  •  HTML5  •  Ludum Dare 28

SAN ANTONIO, TX—The town of Jarlsberg is booming. Residents are flocking and buying up plots of land at a record pace, and it’s up to you, the Mayor, to give these residents the amenities they need in order for Jarlsberg to stay afloat and ensure you don’t go bankrupt. Urban Sprawl began life as a Ludum Dare entry, a contest in which teams of game developers are tasked with creating a game from scratch in 72-hours or less based on a specific theme, in this case being “Evolution.”

9Lives

Dec 2013  •  HTML5  •  Ludum Dare 28

SAN ANTONIO, TX—An adventure that sees the player journey through space, time, and the lands in between on a quest to find Kary the Cat’s missing girlfriend, developed for HTML5 in 72–hours for a game dev challenge. As this was our first attempt at the Ludum Dare, a competition in which developers create a game from scratch in 72 hours, we decided to keep things as simple as possible, while crafting multiple gameplay elements around a compelling story.

Polyplane

Dec 2013  •  HTML5  •  Ludum Dare 28

SAN MARCOS, TX—Taking traditional mazes to the next level, or shall we say levels, Polyplane tasks the player with navigating multi-level mazes in search of the mythical end, travelling through portals that weave their journey back and forth through the endless abyss. In preparation for the Ludum Dare, we decided to do a little warming up, crafting this ultra-simple HTML5 game in less than 6 hours over the course of a late Friday night.
